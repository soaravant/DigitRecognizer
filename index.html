How to use Numberly

  1. Draw a digit (0-9) on the canvas with your mouse or finger.
  2. Pick a model from the left to switch architectures.
  3. Use the brush slider to adjust stroke thickness.
  4. See the predicted digit and top 3 probabilities on the right.
  5. Use the Compare All tab to view outputs from every model.
  6. Press "C" to clear the canvas, "R" to re-run prediction.
